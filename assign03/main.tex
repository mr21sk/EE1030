Question:\\
Find the unit vector in the direction of the vector  $\vec{A}$ = \myvec{1\\1\\2}. 
\\ \\
\solution
The unit vector is given by :-
\begin{align}
	\frac{A}{\norm{A}}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
	\norm{A} &= \sqrt{(A)^T(A)}\\
	&= \sqrt{\myvec{1 & 1 & 2}\myvec{1\\1\\2}}\\
	&= \sqrt{6} 
\end{align}
\\ \\
\centering
\therefore \frac{A}{\norm{A}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{6}} $\myvec{1\\1\\2}$ \\
