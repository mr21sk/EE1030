Question: \\
 Find the area of the region bounded by the ellipse $ \frac{x^2}{4} + \frac{y^2}{9} = 1 $ . 
 \\ \\
\solution \\
using standard equation of ellipse , $ a = 2 $ , $ b = 3 $ 
\\
Area of ellipse is given by :
\begin{align}
                     \pi a b  \\
        \therefore area   &=  \pi \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \\
                          &= 6 \pi 
\end{align}
